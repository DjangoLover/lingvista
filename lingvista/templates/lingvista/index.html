How many times you searched foreign words translations or definitions and couldn't remember after?

Most of dictionary searches are one-off. The key idea of remembering knew words is to repeat them later. And here is where lingvista can help you.

To start learning new words follow steps:
  1. Sing up in our service with social account. By now we support GitHub.
  2. Select your tranlation language in account settings.
  3. Download our Google Chrome Plugin.
  4. Double-click any word you want to translate or define.
  5. Return later to see words you searched and to test yourself whith our trainer.
